Greetings! My name is Joshua Akira Hartigan. I was born in Saint Louis, Missouri and grew up in Potosi, MO for the first twelve years of my life; for the last eight years, in Columbia, MO. I'm a sophomore studying electrical engineering (and tentatively applied math) at Tufts University in Medford, MA. My hobbies include drawing/sketching, reading (mainly historical non-fiction and classics), running (I plan to run the Boston Marathon in 2015...fingers crossed that I survive), and hiking.

One of my biggest wonders in life is our relationship to the ethereal universe. My line of thinking goes: if every human being lives life as the center of their own existence, i.e. of their own universe, what is the contribution of everyone's conscience to the whole of the universe we live in? As Stephen Hawking purports, does the meaning of life created by each sentient individual? The search for truth goes on...
